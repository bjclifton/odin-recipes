How to Make Chocolate Chip Cookies

Following this recipe will yield two dozen of the most delicious delectible cookies you have ever had. This is a classic recipe used my mothers and grandmothers around the world. You will most certainly enjoy it.

Making these cookies is sure to gain you many friends. Everyone will want to come to your house to try these amazing cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
