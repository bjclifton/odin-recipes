Wine-Braised Beef Brisket

How to make Wine-Braised Beef Brisket

This is a very tender, flavor packed brisket that you are sure to love Great with cornbread!

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (3 pound) beef brisket
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can beef broth
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • ½ cup red wine
